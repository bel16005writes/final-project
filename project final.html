Small Beginnings 

From the moment his 6th teacher told him he would never suceed in English, Dominique has taken it upon himself personally to exceed in writing. And he's still trying to prove that teacher wrong.

Growth 

With early beginnings traced back to creating fanfic, all the way to writing essays discussing the ethics of rhetoric. Dominique has worked from the bottom of the written word to the top.     

Now 

Now Dominique writes works on the levels of James Patterson if he actually wrote his books. The New York Times called his work "The next Wordsworth (that's a famous person, right?)." 

2095 

Once the year 2095 rolls around, Dominique plans to never rest until every single one of his works are made into a poor film adaptation, which is then adaptedinto a more well-received TV show.
